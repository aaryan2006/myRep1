Tennis basics

This page will tell you about the basics of tennis


forehand

semi-western grip, by your dominant hand


The forehand is a stroke only for your dominant hand
you have to start with the racket low and quickly bring it
up and make contact with the ball, this is a video of the fore hand, click on the tennis ball .



there are three grips for the forhand

  1. semi-eastern
  2. semi-western
  3. continental

backhand

continental grip, by both your hands or your dominant hand


The backhand can be hit with your dominant hand or both of your hands using the continental grip

serve

continental grip, dominant hand


so in the serve you want to toss the ball high jump and the swing your racket down to the ground
